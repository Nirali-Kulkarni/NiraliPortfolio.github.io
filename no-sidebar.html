Big Data Hadoop Ecosystem

I harnessed the power of Big Data technologies, including Hadoop with HDFS and Hive, coupled with the integration of Hive with Apache Spark and Tableau, to preprocess and reshape extensive data, enabling a comprehensive analysis of a truck fleet dataset. Subsequently, I delivered a compelling presentation of these insightful findings to key stakeholders, with a primary focus on enhancing operational efficiency and safety measure

Snowflake Data Warehouse
